Pita-What?

this blog-site is a small exhibition of mini-projects made by students

their projects reflect ideas, thoughts and inspiration encountered by them, while taking the course "design with code"
patterns, filters, object movement, shadow and light, colors, pixels and vector are some of them

the objective of the course was using code as a thinking tool and medium to create visual objects and to incourge the students, to create new object, while dilling with problems along the way:
the simplest things seems to be quite difficault and chalanging - thats the beauty of coding!

inspiration by art: barbara kruger[1] Endy Warhol[2]

inspiration by nature math and science: Snell's window [1] Erosion [2]
